# 🛠️ Hardware & Transportation Maintenance

**Focus:** ⚡ Electrical | 🛴 Scooters | 🔧 Repairs | 📦 Inventory | 📝 Cleanup & Documentation

## I. Overview
This folder documents the **hardware maintenance, repair, and electronic system hardening** for transportation devices, laptops, and networked hardware. The project focused on both **physical and electrical system improvements**, including battery wiring, inventory management, and operational cleanup. This general project INCLUDES Cybersecurity Hardening and Maintenance found in the first project.

Key objectives:  
- Repair and rewire battery harnesses for electric scooters  
- Maintain and upgrade laptops and computers in the network  
- Manage inventory for parts and electrical components  
- Improve operational efficiency and digital workspace  

---

## II. Inventory & Hardware Systems
- **Computers & Laptops**  
  - 8–10 laptops in the network  
  - Installation of software: Visual Studio Code, Electron, Node.js, JavaScript, Adobe Suite  
  - Free space cleanup: recovered ~50 GB per computer  
- **Transportation Devices**  
  - 3–5 electric scooters  
  - Battery and wiring harness inspection and rewiring  
  - Electrical current monitoring using handheld terminals  
- **Parts & Tools Inventory**  
  - Batteries, wiring harnesses, connectors  
  - Repair and monitoring tools for electrical systems  

---

## III. Procedures & Actions
### A. Computer Systems
- Hard drive cleanup: cleared caches, old files, and unused software (~50 GB recovered)  
- Software installation: Visual Studio Code, Node.js, Electron, Adobe Suite  
- Cybersecurity-related actions: installation of security software, configuration of secure environments  

### B. Transportation & Electrical Systems
- Battery wiring harness repair and rewiring  
- Rewiring and shoring up electrical systems  
- Inspection of ports and connectors for safety and reliability  
- Installation of monitoring devices to track current flow  

### C. General Cleanup & Maintenance
- Organized digital and physical workspace  
- Cleaned devices and peripherals  
- Updated operational documentation for hardware and transportation systems  

---

## IV. Observations & Outcomes
- Recovered free space on computers allows installation of large software suites and more efficient operations  
- Hardware and electrical systems on scooters are now stable and operational  
- Inventory management ensures replacement parts are available when needed  
- Hands-on maintenance and testing improved both reliability and safety of physical and digital systems
